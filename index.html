Our Engagement

During August of 2016, we planned a road trip around a concert at Red Rocks Amphitheater outside of Denver. The first leg of the trip put us at Yellowstone National Park, where we spent time hiking and sightseeing. On our third day in Yellowstone, we got up early and drove out to the northwest area of the park to see buffalo, big horn sheep, elk and bear. Then, with the sun fully up and the beautiful day upon us, we stopped at Tower Falls and hiked along the Yellowstone River with the sunshine streaming down. On that bright, clear morning in Yellowstone, Ryan dropped to one knee, pulled the ring from his camera bag and asked Natalie to be his wife. The rest of the trip became a celebration, from Denver to Southern Utah and back home, we had a spectacular time!
